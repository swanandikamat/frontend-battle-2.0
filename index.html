About Frontend Battle

Frontend Battle is a coding competition that challenges developers to replicate modern UI designs using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's a creative space to learn, compete, and innovate.

Features

Live Challenges

Compete with other developers on real-world frontend tasks.

Leaderboard

Track your progress and climb the ranks.

See how your designs compare to the originals in real time.
